- **Highlight:** Easy CRUD operations, visual indicators

## üé¨ Demo Script Template

### Opening (30 sec)
> "Today I'm showing you SmartPantry - an MVP that helps people manage their pantry, reduce food waste, and plan meals smarter. It's built as a mobile-first Progressive Web App, so it works on any device."

### Core Features (4-5 min)
> "Let me walk you through the key features:
> 
> **First, the dashboard** - gives you instant visibility into what's expiring or running low.
> 
> **Pantry management** - easily add items, see what's expiring, track quantities.
> 
> **AI meal suggestions** - this is where it gets smart. The app uses OpenAI to suggest meals based on what you actually have, considering your dietary preferences. Notice the detailed recipes with ingredient amounts and a multiplier to scale recipes.
> 
> **Weekly planning** - assign saved meals to days of the week for meal prep.
> 
> **Smart grocery lists** - automatically add expiring or low-stock items, or build manually. You can copy or share lists easily.
> 
> **Personalization** - set dietary restrictions, allergies, and notification preferences."

### Closing (30 sec)
> "This MVP demonstrates the core value proposition: reduce food waste through better inventory management and AI-powered meal planning. The tech stack is Node.js, Express, Prisma, PostgreSQL, and Next.js with PWA support for mobile installation."
